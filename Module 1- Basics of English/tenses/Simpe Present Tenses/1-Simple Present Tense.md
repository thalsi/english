## 2. STRUCTURE

| Sentence Type     | Subjects         | Structure                  | Example                    |
| ----------------- | ---------------- | -------------------------- | -------------------------- |
| Positive          | I, You, We, They | Subject + V1               | We play cricket.           |
| Positive          | He, She, It      | Subject + V1 + s/es        | He plays cricket.          |
| Negative          | I, You, We, They | Subject + do not + V1      | They do not play cricket.  |
| Negative          | He, She, It      | Subject + does not + V1    | She does not play cricket. |
| Positive Question | I, You, We, They | Do + subject + V1?         | Do you play cricket?       |
| Positive Question | He, She, It      | Does + subject + V1?       | Does he play cricket?      |
| Negative Question | I, You, We, They | Do + subject + not + V1?   | Do they not play cricket?  |
| Negative Question | He, She, It      | Does + subject + not + V1? | Does she not play cricket? |
