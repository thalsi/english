# English Full Course Outline

## Module 1: Basics of English

* Alphabet & Pronunciation (A–Z, vowels & consonants)
* Numbers, Days, Months, Dates, Time
* Greetings, Introductions & Common Phrases
* Basic Sentence Structure (Subject + Verb + Object)
* Nouns, Pronouns & Articles (a, an, the)
* Simple Questions & Negatives
* Common Prepositions (in, on, at, under, etc.)
* Common Adjectives & Adverbs
* Basic Vocabulary (home, family, school, food, colors)
* Listening & Speaking practice with simple conversations

## Module 2: Tenses

* Present Tense

  * Simple Present
  * Present Continuous
  * Present Perfect
  * Present Perfect Continuous
* Past Tense

  * Simple Past
  * Past Continuous
  * Past Perfect
  * Past Perfect Continuous
* Future Tense

  * Simple Future
  * Future Continuous
  * Future Perfect
  * Future Perfect Continuous
* Using Tenses in Writing & Speaking
* Time Expressions (always, yesterday, tomorrow, etc.)
* Exercises & Conversation Practice

## Module 3: Grammar Essentials

* Parts of Speech (noun, pronoun, verb, adjective, adverb, preposition, conjunction, interjection)
* Subject-Verb Agreement
* Sentence Types (Affirmative, Negative, Interrogative, Exclamatory)
* Active & Passive Voice
* Direct & Indirect Speech
* Modals (can, could, should, must, might, will)
* Conditional Sentences (if-clauses)
* Question Forms & Tag Questions
* Comparatives & Superlatives
* Punctuation (full stop, comma, colon, semi-colon, quotation marks)

## Module 4: Vocabulary & Usage

* Daily Life Vocabulary (shopping, travel, school, work)
* Synonyms & Antonyms
* Idioms & Phrases
* Collocations (make a decision, take a break)
* Homophones & Homonyms
* Commonly Confused Words (their/there/they’re, your/you’re)
* Word Formation (prefixes & suffixes)
* Slang & Informal Expressions

## Module 5: Reading Skills

* Reading Simple Sentences & Paragraphs
* Comprehension Skills (finding main idea, details)
* Skimming & Scanning Techniques
* Reading Stories, News Articles & Dialogues
* Understanding Context & Inference
* Vocabulary from Reading

## Module 6: Writing Skills

* Sentence Writing
* Paragraph Writing
* Letter & Email Writing (formal & informal)
* Essay Writing
* Story Writing
* Dialogue Writing
* Summarizing & Paraphrasing
* Writing Practice for Speaking (scripts, conversations)

## Module 7: Speaking Skills

* Pronunciation & Accent Training
* Common Conversation Practice
* Introducing Yourself & Others
* Asking & Answering Questions
* Giving Opinions & Suggestions
* Role Plays & Dialogues
* Public Speaking Basics
* Speaking on Everyday Topics
* Debate & Discussion Practice

## Module 8: Listening Skills

* Listening to Conversations & Dialogues
* Understanding Instructions
* Listening to Stories & News
* Listening for Details vs. General Ideas
* Note-taking Skills
* Listening Exercises with Speaking Practice

## Module 9: Advanced English

* Advanced Grammar (Subjunctive, Relative Clauses, Inversion)
* Advanced Vocabulary & Phrasal Verbs
* Formal vs. Informal English
* Idioms & Proverbs in Daily Speech
* Advanced Reading Comprehension
* Creative Writing & Storytelling
* Presentation Skills
* Interview Practice
* Writing Reports & Articles

## Module 10: Exam & Daily Use Practice

* English Tests Practice (Grammar, Reading, Listening, Writing)
* Real-life Conversation Practice
* Watching English Movies & News
* Reading Books & Newspapers
* Speaking with Friends / Online Partners
* Continuous Writing Practice
